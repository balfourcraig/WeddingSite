Payments & Money

Most places won't accept Visa/Mastercard. Some big hotels or attractions might, but the average shop won't.

Most people in China use WeChat to communicate and pay for things, but the payments are difficult to set up without a Chinese bank account.

You can use either cash, or AliPay - which is a bit like Google/Apple pay here.
